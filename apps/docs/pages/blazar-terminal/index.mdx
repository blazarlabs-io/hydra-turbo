# Blazar Terminal

**BLE-powered contactless payment terminal for the Blazar Pay ecosystem**

The Blazar Terminal is a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) payment terminal that enables contactless micropayments using Cardano blockchain technology. It communicates with mobile devices over BLE and with merchant apps over WebSocket to facilitate secure, low-latency payments (ADA and custom assets).
